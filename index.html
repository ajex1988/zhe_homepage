About me

I'm a Research Engineer from Irvine, California. My research interests cover a range of topics in Computer Vision and Computer Graphics. I work as Staff Engineer in Samsung Research America.

I was born in the central part of China, where the most famous place nearby is Wudang Mountain, known as the holy land of Taoism. Spicy food is popular in my hometown. I got my undergraduate degree from the School of Computer Science at Wuhan University and then got my PhD at the Department of Computer Science, Tsinghua University. My supervisor is Prof. Shimin Hu.
